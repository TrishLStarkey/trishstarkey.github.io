About

I’m Trish Starkey. I work at the intersection of strategy, commercial execution, and people systems, helping leaders navigate growth and change without losing clarity or momentum.

In my current role as Chief of Staff and Chief People Officer at an energy infrastructure venture, I partner closely with executives on strategy execution, stakeholder communications, and operating cadence. My background spans commercial strategy, marketing and positioning, and leadership enablement in complex, long-cycle B2B environments.

I’m a Columbia University–certified executive coach (3CP). My approach blends rigor and empathy. Clients describe me as clear, grounded, and practical. We focus on sharpening thinking, tightening the story leaders tell about their work, and building execution systems that scale with responsibility.

I’m selective about new engagements to ensure each client receives focused, high-quality support.
